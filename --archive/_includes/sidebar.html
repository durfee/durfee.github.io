About

I'm Sean Durfee (dur•fee), a Vancouver, BC based multi-disciplinary designer. I'm a project manager, designer, front-end developer, and jack-of-all-trades with Denim and Steel. I hold a degree in International Relations from the University of British Columbia with a focus in international security, East Asia, trade and languages (English, French & Italian with mild proficiency in Mandarin and Spanish). I'm a generalist designer that works across many disciplines – a fusionist, if you're feeling buzz-wordy.

On the side, I build community content for #Startup604, lead product design for my company, Chilly Technologies, a swiss army knife, resident fire extinguisher and designer with the Vancouver Design Week team. I like to keep busy.

A few of my past clients: TRIBE, The Envoy, Friends Made, The University of British Columbia, Commissary Connect, UBC's IRSA, and more.

You can find me on the internet on Twitter, Github, Instagram, Spotify, and Behance. Or send me an email.
