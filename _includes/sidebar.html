About

I'm Sean Durfee (dur•fee), a Vancouver, BC based multi-disciplinary designer. I hold a degree in International Relations with a focus in international security, East Asia, trade and languages (English, French & Italian with mild proficiency in Mandarin and Spanish). I'm a generalist designer that works across many disciplines. I have experience with project management, product design, branding, web design, photography, and a dash of video.

I'm in between gigs right now so you can hire me! (Pleeez). On the side, I build community content for #Startup604 and lead product design for my company, Chilly Technologies.

Past clients include: TRIBE, The Envoy, Friends Made, Iona Journal of Economics, The University of British Columbia, Commissary Connect, TARS Technology, UBC's IRSA, and more.

I like people. We'd probably get along. You can find me on the internet on Twitter, Github, Instagram, Spotify, and Behance. Or send me an email.
